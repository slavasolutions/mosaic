# 2024 in numbers

This year, Hromada grew from a small Saturday gathering into a year-round community space. Here's how it looked.

## What changed

We opened our downtown location in March, launched language classes in May, and held our first cultural festival in September. We hired our first full-time settlement coordinator in November.

## What's next

In 2025, we're expanding language programs and starting a youth mentorship initiative.
