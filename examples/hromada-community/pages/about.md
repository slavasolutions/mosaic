# About us

Hromada was founded in 2022 by a group of newcomers and long-settled Ukrainian-Canadians who saw what was missing: a single place where settlement help, language learning, and cultural life could happen together.

## How we work

We operate out of a community space in downtown Toronto. Most of our programs are volunteer-led. We partner with local libraries, schools, and faith communities to extend our reach.

## Where the name comes from

*Hromada* (громада) is the Ukrainian word for "community" — specifically, the kind of community that takes care of itself.
