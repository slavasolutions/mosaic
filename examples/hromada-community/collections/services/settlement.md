# Settlement support

Arriving in a new country is a hundred decisions in the first month — schools, doctors, leases, ID, banking. Our settlement coordinators help newcomer families work through that list, in Ukrainian or English.

## What's included

- Initial intake and needs assessment
- Help with housing search and lease review
- Connections to healthcare and family doctors
- School enrollment for children
- Government ID, SIN, OHIP applications
- Banking and tax setup

## How to access

Walk in Tuesdays and Thursdays, 10am-4pm. No appointment needed. Or email us to book a time.
