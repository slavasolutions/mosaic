# Spring festival recap

Over 500 people joined us at Trinity Bellwoods Park for a day of music, food, and dance. Thank you to every volunteer, performer, and family who made it happen.

## Highlights

The Bandura ensemble from the Ukrainian Youth Association performed three pieces. The vyshyvanka workshop sold out within an hour. The food tent served 800 varenyky.
