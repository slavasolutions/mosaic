# Welcome to Glasshouse

We're a two-person letterpress and book studio in southeast Portland. We design, set, print, and bind small editions — usually fewer than 300 copies, sometimes fewer than 30. Most of what we make is poetry, field guides, and the occasional artist monograph.

This site is the studio's running record: what's on press now, what's shipped, who works on what, and the longer pieces we write when something deserves more than a caption.
