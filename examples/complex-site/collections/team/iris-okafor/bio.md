## How Iris got here

Iris apprenticed at a trade bindery in Chicago for four years out of art school, then spent six years freelance binding for small presses across the Midwest. She moved to Portland in 2018 looking for cheaper rent and an old letterpress; she found both within a month.

## What she does now

Iris runs the press, does most of the binding by hand, and handles the books-as-physical-objects side of the studio. She's the one to email if you want to commission a project.
