# Sasha Ren

## Background

Sasha trained as a graphic designer in Seoul and Vancouver before coming to letterpress through a single workshop at a community studio in 2017. She was supposed to be there for a weekend; she stayed eighteen months.

## At the press

Sasha runs design and typesetting and operates the Heidelberg windmill. She's the reason our title pages look like title pages instead of book covers. She also fixes most things that break, including the guillotine, which breaks often.
