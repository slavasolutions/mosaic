# Production notes

Issue 03 runs to 96 pages, perfect-bound, with a four-color cover and a two-color interior (warm black plus the Atrium signature ochre). The text is Source Serif at 10/14; folios are Inter at 8.

The big change from Issue 02 is the paper: we moved from a brightwhite coated stock to Hahnemühle Biblio, an uncoated text paper. It absorbs ink differently — halftones lose detail in shadows and you can't run as much density on solids — but the long essays read better, which is what the editors wanted.
