# The Hahnemühle order finally arrived

Three months late, two cartons short, and the wrong shade of cream on the cover stock. Otherwise perfect.

We ordered this paper for *Atrium Quarterly* back in May, and at the time the lead time was eight weeks. It became ten, then twelve, then "we'll call you." It arrived this morning by truck, the driver apologetic, the boxes clearly re-taped at customs.

The good news: the text stock — Hahnemühle Biblio 100gsm — is exactly what we wanted. Warm white, faint laid texture, takes ink like it was waiting for it. The bad news: the cover, which was supposed to be a soft ecru, came in nearly bone-white. We'll either re-order (another twelve weeks) or shift the design to lean into the cooler tone. Sasha's in the studio now mocking up both versions.

## What we learned

Order the paper before you design the book. This is the third time we've said this. We will probably say it again.
